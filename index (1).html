How it works

Study. Simulate. Score. Repeat.

Every chapter follows a proven game loop that turns exam objectives into immersive field experience.

01

Read the field manual

Each chapter starts with an interactive study guide — clickable cards, embedded quizzes, term-matching games, and exam tips woven into the narrative.

02

Deploy to the simulation

Get dispatched to a realistic scenario — a downed server, a network outage, a security breach. Use your toolbelt and troubleshoot under pressure.

03

Hidden checklist scoring

You don't see objectives during the sim. Afterward, the full checklist reveals what you nailed and what you missed — like a real performance review.

04

Earn XP & unlock missions

Star ratings, XP accumulation, and rank progression unlock the next chapter. Replay any mission to improve your score and climb the ranks.
